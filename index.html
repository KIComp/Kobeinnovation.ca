About Kobe Innovation Company

Kobe Innovation Company is a pioneering indoor farming venture dedicated to cultivating premium-grade saffron. Using advanced multilayer vertical farming techniques, we optimize space and achieve multiple harvests per year. Our mission is to become a leading Canadian producer of high-quality saffron, supplying wholesalers, the beauty industry, and large manufacturers with a consistent, locally grown product. We envision becoming a trusted name in agriculture, recognized for our superior quality and innovative methods.

Our Saffron

Our saffron is grown in a state-of-the-art indoor facility, ensuring consistent quality and purity. Hand-harvested and carefully dried, it retains its rich flavor, aroma, and vibrant color. Ideal for culinary uses, cosmetic formulations, and pharmaceutical applications, our saffron meets the highest standards of excellence.

How We Grow

We employ cutting-edge techniques to cultivate saffron indoors:

  • Controlled Environment: Precise management of temperature, light, and humidity to induce multiple flowering cycles.
  • Batch Management: Staggered planting for continuous production.
  • Vertical Farming: Multilayer racks maximize space and efficiency.

Our Markets

We serve B2B clients in the following sectors:

  • Wholesalers: Bulk distributors seeking premium saffron for resale.
  • Beauty Industry: Manufacturers of saffron-infused skincare products.
  • Large Manufacturers: Food and beverage companies incorporating saffron into their offerings.

Our competitive advantages include local production, consistent quality, and the ability to deliver multiple harvests per year.
