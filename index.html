Introducing OxyCycle: The Future of Urban Air Purification

OxyCycle is our flagship product designed to transform urban spaces into cleaner, healthier, and greener environments. These 500-liter algae biopurifiers use cutting-edge microalgae technology to remove CO₂ from the atmosphere while releasing oxygen, mimicking the natural purification processes of trees.

How OxyCycle Works

  1. CO₂ Absorption: Ambient air is drawn into the tank using a solar-powered air pump. Microalgae (*Spirulina* or *Chlorella*) inside the tank absorb CO₂ and use it in photosynthesis.
  2. Oxygen Release: The photosynthesis process releases pure oxygen, which diffuses into the atmosphere through semi-open tank systems.
  3. Self-Sustaining Energy: Solar panels power the air pump and LED grow lights, ensuring round-the-clock operation. Backup batteries store excess energy for use during cloudy days.
  4. Maintenance-Friendly: Built-in self-cleaning filters prevent algae buildup and ensure clean water circulation.
  5. Cold Climate Adaptation: Submersible heaters maintain optimal water temperatures (20–30°C), making OxyCycle operational even during extreme Winnipeg winters.
  6. Community Engagement: Tanks feature informational displays explaining the science behind OxyCycle, educating passersby about climate action.

OxyCycle’s Key Features

  • Durable and Aesthetic Design: Custom circular acrylic tanks designed to blend seamlessly into public spaces.
  • Low Maintenance: Self-cleaning systems reduce manual intervention to biannual maintenance.
  • Carbon Offset: Each tank removes 30–40 kg of CO₂ annually, the equivalent of the air purification provided by several mature trees.
  • Year-Round Functionality: Equipped to handle Canada’s extreme winters, OxyCycle performs in both indoor and outdoor settings.
